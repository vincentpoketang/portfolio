About Vincent

About 10 years ago, I took my first class in computer science at high school and learned the basics of programming. Although in later years, the class series ended up being removed from the school, I was amazed by all the things that you can make with just a few lines of code. It fueled my passion to learn more on the subject and drove me to major in Computer Science at college.

Fast-forward to recent years, I graduated from UC Irvine with a major in Computer Science (Specialized in Intelligent System) and enrolled in LearningFuze, an accelerated web development program, to further my knowledge in web development.

As a recent graduate from UC Irvine and LearningFuze, I want to bring my passion, professional work ethics and knowledge for programming into my new career!
